# Assets

**Art direction:** Vibrant 16-bit SNES-style pixel art. Rich color palettes per era: deep blues/greens for ocean, lush greens/browns for swamp/forest, warm oranges/reds for desert/volcanic, verdant greens for dinosaur era, cool whites/blues for ice age. Top-down perspective with tile-based maps. Creatures are small animated sprites (32x32 to 48x48). Combat view is first-person with large enemy portraits. UI is clean retro with pixel fonts.
